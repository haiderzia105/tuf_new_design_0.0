BROUCHERS

Brochures are developed to provide the information about the undergraduate and postgraduate degree programs within each discipline, campus and student life, accommodation, entry requirements and available scholarships for the new aspirants at The University of Faisalabad. Various Schools and Departments also used to develop brochure as a source of information and promoting for their individual academic and co-curricular activities and programs throughout the year.

These are also developed to provide an introduction and information about the seminars and conferences and other research activities under the umbrella of Office of Research Innovation and Commercialization (ORIC) at The University of Faisalabad.
